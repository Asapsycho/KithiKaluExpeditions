MASAI MARA - Day 1 ; Departure from Nairobi around 6:00 am. Around 9.30 in the morning we will arrive at the entrance to the most beautiful nature reserve in Kenya: the Masai Mara. The name derives from the Masai people (who live in various areas of the plain) and from the Mara river. From July to September it is possible to admire the unique spectacle of the wildebeest migration from the Serengeti to the Masai Mara. This is the only park where

you can spot all 5 of the "Big Five" of the African savannah: lion, elephant, buffalo, leopard and rhinoceros. Furthermore, here is the Talek and Mara river, populated by numerous hippos, Maasai giraffes, zebras, gazelles of different breeds, antelopes and other animals typical of this unique reserve. We will arrive at the lodge/camp around 12pm, here we will be able to have lunch and rest until 4pm, when we will leave for the afternoon game drive. Return to our lodge/camp for dinner and overnight.

MASAI MARA - Day 2 ; Around 6:00 breakfast and, having taken our lunch boxes, we set off again. This day will also be spent entirely on game drives in the park in search of as many emotions as possible.
Lunch will always take place in a place of our choice in the shade, where we can rest a bit before leaving for the afternoon safari. After sunset, return to the lodge/camp for overnight stay.

NAKURU - Day 3 ; 6:00 am breakfast and departure to leave the Masai Mara and arrive at the Lake Nakuru reserve. The origin of the name Nakuru comes from the Masai language and has the meaning of "dust" or "dusty place", originally used for the city of the same name.
The national park (188 km2 in size) was established in 1961, around the lake of the same name, essentially to protect the very large colony of pink flamingos that lived here feeding on microcrustaceans and algae. The number of flamingos varies based on the amount of food, related to the salinity of the water.
Game drive in the morning until you arrive at the lodge, where you will be assigned rooms and have lunch. At around 3.45pm we set off again for the afternoon game drive and return to the camp/lodge for dinner and overnight stay.

NAIROBI - Day 4 ; 6:00 am breakfast and departure for game drive and return to Nairobi, where we will arrive towards . Depending on the continuation of your holiday, from here it is possible to take a domestic flight that will take you to Mombasa or Malindi, or return to Italy. Times can be adjusted according to your needs.

A tour in the most beautiful reserve in Kenya passing through the breathtaking landscapes of Lake Nakuru starting from Nairobi.
