Group Trips

Enjoy

You will be able to plant mangroves with the local community,
meet the Mijikenda tribe and their ancestral traditions,
spot dolphins in the ocean and sleep in one of the most exclusive accommodations in the savannah.

Everything will be coordinated on site by KithiKaluExpeditions.

You will stay in a hotel with an internal restaurant a stone's throw from the sea where
you will experience exciting adventures organized by the multi-award-winning local Tripadvisor partner agency KithiKaluExpeditions.

Dates to be defined:
CONTACT US FOR MORE INFOs

Day 1 – Relax and tour the local markets by
tuk tuk

Day 2 – Departure at 9am for a day in a dhow on the ocean with fish grill, snorkelling on the coral reef and dolphin watching

Day 3 – Departure at 6am for safari in the Tsavo East park with overnight stay at Voi Wildlife lodge

Day 4 – Safari in the Tsavo East and Taita Hills parks with overnight stay in Sarova Salt Lick (EXTRA: possibility to participate in a night safari, bookable directly on site)

Day 5 – Safari in Taita Hills park and return to Watamu in the evening

Day 6 – Free morning, in the afternoon visit to Malindi and its surroundings, Marafa and its famous canyon at sunset

Day 7 – Departure at 9am for cycling: we will spend half a day with the traditional Giriama tribe. Free afternoon, 6.30 pm cooking class + typical African dinner

Day 8 – Free day with activities to be defined on site based on the departure time of the flight from Mombasa
