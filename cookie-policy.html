Cookie Policy

This cookie policy (“ Cookie Policy ”) is aimed at informing the User in relation to the use of cookies by the kithikaluexpeditions website (“ Site ”).
Cookies are used to make the experience on the Site simpler and more intuitive, to offer more pleasant and efficient navigation and to store and transmit certain information
relating to the User.


WHAT ARE COOKIES?

 Cookies are small text files sent by the Site to the User's device (" Device ") and stored on the latter, to be re-transmitted on each subsequent visit.


Types of cookies

  Cookies are usually divided into two macro categories: technical cookies and profiling cookies

  Furthermore, cookies can be classified according to the entity that operates as data controller of the personal data collected by the cookie,
  distinguishing between cookies coming from the site visited (so-called first-party cookies) and third-party cookies

TECHNICAL COOKIES

 Technical cookies are used for the sole purpose of optimizing your navigation on the Site and allowing us to provide the User with the requested services.


 Technical cookies are generally divided into navigation cookies, functionality cookies and analytical cookies. In particular:

 1.Browsing cookies are essential to offer an optimal experience to the User or to allow them to authenticate on the Site. Browsing cookies are normally session cookies and therefore once the browsing browser is closed, they are deleted.
 KithiKaluExpeditions uses navigation cookies.
 2.analytical cookies ( or analytics ) are used to collect statistical information, in anonymous and aggregate form, on the number of users who access the Site and
 the related methods of use and interaction.
 3.functionality cookies are used for the purpose of memorizing some User preferences (for example, the language or country of origin), without having to reset them during subsequent visits. Functionality cookies are often persistent cookies
 as they remain stored in the Device even after the browser is closed, until their expiry date or until the User decides to delete them. KithiKaluExpeditions uses functionality cookies;


PROFILING COOKIES

Profiling cookies are aimed at creating profiles relating to the User to track their behavior and are used in order to offer services such as,
for example the sending of advertising messages in line with the preferences expressed by the same while browsing the website.


COOKIE MANAGEMENT

 Deactivating technical cookies could compromise the functioning of the Site and/or limit the offering of services.

 Each browser manages cookies differently and most accept them automatically. However, you can change your browser settings to disable this setting.

 To manage your preferences in relation to cookies, follow the instructions for the browser you use to browse the Site: Internet Explorer, Chrome, Opera, Safari, Firefox.


COOKIE POLICY UPDATE

 KithiKalu Expeditions, at its discretion and at any time may change, modify, add or remove parts of the Cookie Policy.
 The User is invited to periodically check this Cookie Policy. Use of the Site following the publication of changes to the Cookie Policy entails full acceptance.
