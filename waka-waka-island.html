Waka island

Glass bottom boat, equipped for snorkeling and raised roof

Authorized local guide with regular license (in Italian)

Entrance fee to Watamu Marine Park

Fish-based lunch (possibility of meat or vegetarian), non-alcoholic drinks

1st ; Departure around 08:00 from your accommodation to go and take a glass bottom boat equipped with a raised roof where you can sunbathe during the journey.

First stop: Watamu marine park, a protected natural reserve where you can snorkel in search of the thousand tropical fish that inhabit the coral reef with masks, fins and life jackets (they can be provided by us).

Dolphin watching (from November to March, if the season allows it)

Journey to Sardinia 2 (Jacaranda beach).

2nd; Once you arrive in this heavenly atoll, visible only at low tide, you can admire the crystal clear water with a thousand shades from transparent to blue, relax, walk, sunbathe and look for starfish while waiting for lunch which will be served to you on the boat: coconut rice, octopus sauce, lobsters, freshly grilled fish and prawns, fruit and non-alcoholic drinks..

After lunch, some relaxation before returning to your accommodation in the late afternoon.

Duration: half day (can only be organized according to the tide).
